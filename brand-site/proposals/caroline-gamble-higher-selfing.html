Where I'd Start

The First Bite

Before any of the bigger stuff, I want to knock out the thing that will give you the most time back immediately: get you onto Stripe and automate your scheduling and payments.

1

Scheduling System

I'd set you up with Calendly where you control your available slots — no clients before 10 AM, RTT sessions 10-12:30, regular sessions in the afternoon, whatever you want. Clients get a link, they see what's open, they book. No more double-booking, no more back-and-forth. You can still send your Sunday Ponder and just include the booking link — same personal touch, zero manual scheduling.

2

Stripe for Payments

Clients put a card on file. You can auto-charge on a schedule — monthly for packages, per-session, before the 24-hour cancellation window, however you want to structure it. No more Venmo requests. No more chasing payments. It's professional, it's clean, and lowers friction for clients.

3

Confirmation & Reminder Flow

When a client books, they get an automatic confirmation. They get a reminder before their session. If they need to reschedule, they can do it through the system within your cancellation policy. You're not managing any of this manually.

This alone probably saves you 3-5+ hours a week.

And immediately makes your business feel more professional — which matters when you've got an influencer ready to "explode" you and clients telling you they can't find where to book.

I'd do this one for you as a friend and as a solid for Mark. No charge. Just let me take this off your plate and show you what's possible.
