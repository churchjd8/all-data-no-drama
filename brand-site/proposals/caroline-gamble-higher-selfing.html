Where I'd Start

The First Bite

Before any of the bigger stuff, I want to knock out the thing that will give you the most time back immediately: get you onto Stripe and automate your scheduling and payments.

You said it yourself — every non-chargeable hour you spend on admin is an hour you could be with a client. Right now you're probably losing 3-5+ hours a week to manual scheduling, Venmo requests, and confirmation texts. That's real money, and it's the easiest thing to fix.

1

Scheduling System

I'd set you up with Calendly where you control your available slots — no clients before 10 AM, RTT sessions 10-12:30, regular sessions in the afternoon, whatever you want. Clients get a link, they see what's open, they book. No more double-booking, no more back-and-forth. You can still send your Sunday Ponder and just include the booking link — same personal touch, zero manual scheduling.

2

Stripe for Payments

Clients put a card on file. You can auto-charge on a schedule — monthly for Higher Selfing Private clients, per-session for drop-ins, or upfront for Higher Self Recode programs. No more Venmo requests. No more chasing payments. It's professional, it's clean, and lowers friction for clients. And like your coach in Australia — when clients are auto-charged at the start of the month, they show up differently.

3

Confirmation & Reminder Flow

When a client books, they get an automatic confirmation. They get a reminder before their session. The 24-hour cancellation policy is enforced automatically — payment is collected before that window. If they need to reschedule, they do it through the system. You're not managing any of this manually.

This alone probably saves you 3-5+ hours a week — and recovers capacity for 1-2 more client sessions.

It also immediately makes your business feel ready for what's coming. You've got an influencer who wants to explode you, clients who can't find where to book, and a $10K/week target on the table. The infrastructure has to match the work.

I'd do this one for you as a friend and as a solid for Mark. No charge. Just let me take this off your plate and show you what's possible.
